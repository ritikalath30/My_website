Real-Time Sign Language Recognition
Learning sign language is often deemed difficult, it's essential for communication with those with disabilities. Sign language detection systems offer a solution. Our study introduces a multilayer Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model to identify ten American Sign Language (ASL) signs. Trained on a robust dataset, the model achieves 95% validation accuracy. This CNN architecture precisely recognizes and classifies ASL hand gestures, empowering disabled individuals for effective communication
Slang Language Detection and Identification
This research paper focuses on detecting and identifying internet slang in modern communication, crucial for understanding social media texts. By employing various techniques, the study achieved a 95.37% accuracy rate in spotting slang words in sentences. Accurate identification enhances our grasp of online conversations, emphasizing the importance of this skill in interpreting the evolving linguistic landscape of digital communication.
Enhancing Mental Health Care: A Proposed Approach for Depression Detection using CNN and LSTM Model
This project uses a hybrid deep learning model combining CNN and LSTM to detect depression from text and visual inputs like social media posts. CNN captures spatial features, while LSTM analyzes language patterns over time. The goal is to support early mental health diagnosis through data-driven insights, with a strong focus on ethical use and user privacy.
PAWnnect: Pioneering IoT-ML Driven Pet Monitoring”
PAWnnect is a smart pet monitoring system that combines IoT devices with Machine Learning to track vital signs like temperature, heart rate, and movement in real time. Using wearable sensors and ML algorithms, it detects health anomalies early and alerts owners through a user-friendly dashboard. The solution enhances pet safety and wellness by blending intelligent analytics with connected care.
Introduction to Generative AI
Generative AI (Gen AI) is revolutionizing content creation by producing human-like text, images, and media using models like OpenAI’s GPT. Predicted to generate 10% of global data by 2025 (Gartner, 2022), it’s reshaping industries while raising concerns around ethics, privacy, and authorship. From GANs to LLMs, Gen AI offers major productivity gains but also poses challenges that demand responsible use.
